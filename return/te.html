The Quantum Ticking Clock

The most immediate quantum threat isn't that a quantum computer will be built tomorrow. It's that adversaries are capturing and storing our encrypted data **today**.

"Harvest Now, Decrypt Later"

Think of your most sensitive data—financial records, intellectual property, national security secrets—being stolen and stored in a locked box. While safe today, the key to unlock that box is a quantum computer. The theft is happening now; the damage comes later. The only defense is to build a better lock.

Solutions in Action

We provide tangible, quantum-resilient solutions for the world's most critical sectors.

Securing a Drone Fleet's Communications

SECTOR: DEFENSE

Problem: Command and control signals for autonomous drones are vulnerable to "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" attacks, risking future mission compromise.

Solution: We deploy lightweight, hardware-accelerated PQC algorithms that provide robust security with minimal impact on the drone's size, weight, and power (SWaP) constraints.

Protecting Implantable Medical Device Data

SECTOR: HEALTHCARE

Problem: Patient data transmitted from devices like pacemakers must remain confidential for decades, but the devices themselves have extreme power limitations.

Solution: Our hybrid architecture offloads heavy PQC operations to a patient's smartphone, using ultra-efficient symmetric keys for real-time communication, ensuring both security and long battery life.

Future-Proofing a Global Payment Network

SECTOR: FINANCE

Problem: A vast network of legacy and modern POS terminals needs to transition to PQC without disrupting service or compromising security.

Solution: Our crypto-agile platform allows for a phased migration, enabling new PQC-enabled terminals to remain backward-compatible with older systems, ensuring a seamless and secure transition.

At Moontrace, we understand that early-stage investment is not just about capital – it's a partnership of vision, belief, and long-term conviction. We deeply value the trust our investors place in us, especially in an emerging domain like Post-Quantum Cryptography.

To reflect that trust with fairness, transparency, and alignment, we have adopted a structured, founder-conscious equity approach.

Our Funding Instrument: ISAFE Notes (Cap + Discount)

We prefer to use the 100x VC's Standard ISAFE (India Simple Agreement for Future Equity) for all early-stage investment rounds, featuring:

  • Valuation Cap: Ensures that your early belief is rewarded – you'll convert equity at a fair ceiling, even if the company's valuation grows rapidly. This protects early investors by setting a maximum valuation at which their investment converts into equity.
  • Discount Rate: As an early backer, you'll receive a 10-20% discount on shares compared to new investors in our priced round. This further rewards early-stage risk-takers by allowing them to purchase shares at a lower price than future investors.

This structure simplifies early financing, reduces legal complexity, and aligns incentives – allowing us to focus intensely on product development, team building, and achieving market traction, rather than getting bogged down in complex legal negotiations.
