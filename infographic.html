Strategic Imperatives

Developing a truly "lightweight" PQC algorithm for defense requires a holistic strategy.

1.

Co-Design Hardware & Software

Design algorithms that are explicitly suited for hardware acceleration on FPGAs and RISC-V from day one.

2.

Obsess Over Data Footprint

Prioritize minimal key and signature sizes to reduce network load. Every byte counts on tactical radios.

3.

Embrace Crypto-Agility

Build systems that can easily swap algorithms to adapt to new threats and standards. Don't get locked in.

4.

Use Granular Security

Match the security level to the data's sensitivity. A "one-size-fits-all" approach is inefficient and impractical.
